About me

My name is Yiying Zou and I'm a front-end web developer based in Toronto.

I got into coding in middle school by building anime fansites as a hobby, but left it behind to pursue drawing and painting. I combine my background in fine arts, teaching, and client services with my natural curiosity to face new challenges and situations.

I love the challenge of using code to build aesthetically-pleasing websites while solving problems. As a motivated self-learner, I am excited by the challenges and rewards or using code to solve problems.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me drawing in my sketchbook or dancing to some 90s tunes.
